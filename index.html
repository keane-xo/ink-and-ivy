before you borrow

borrowing guidelines

these limits help make sure everyone gets a turn with the books they love.

3

books at a time

you may have up to three pending checkout requests or active loans at once.

2

new titles every 9 weeks

you may suggest up to two books to be added to the library during any rolling nine-week period.

14

days to read

each approved checkout is due two weeks after it is approved.

the two-per-nine-weeks limit is only for suggesting new titles to add to the library. it does not limit checkout requests for books already on the shelves.
